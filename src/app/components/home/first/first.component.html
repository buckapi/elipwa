About my

Capturing Life's Precious Moments

My camera is an extension of my vision to discover and celebrate joy, love and togetherness, making each image tell a unique and personal story. Let me be a part of your most precious moments, capturing them for you to treasure forever. I am of Salvadorian origin and fluent in English and Spanish, my multicultural perspective enriches my photography. Married and a mother of three, with three grandchildren, I understand the importance of every moment in life. With my camera, I capture with love and authenticity from the sweetness of a newborn to the joy of a birthday, preserving your most precious memories. Let me tell your story through my images.
